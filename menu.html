BBQ Chicken

Make the BBQ Chicken sandwich your game day go to. It’s mouthwatering, sweet and tangy and made with St. Louis style BBQ sauce. Tastes great with lettuce, tomatoes, red onion and pickles.

Buffalo Chicken

Nothing says football season like Buffalo Chicken, and nothing says buffalo like Frank’s RedHot®. So order up the new, spicy Buffalo Chicken sandwich made with everyone’s favorite sauce—Frank’s RedHot®. Try it with lettuce, tomatoes and cucumbers!

Black Forest Ham

Black Forest Ham sandwich is classic. Just add your own flavor. Oh, and it's the healthiet sandwich we got with two servings of crisp veggies on freshly baked bread for under 400 calories.
